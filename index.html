About Me

Hello! I'm Connor Howe, a PhD student specializing in quantum communications and distributed quantum computing. My research focuses on optimizing quantum-classical hybrid systems and designing scalable quantum networks.

Research

My primary research interests include:

  • Dynamic routing in quantum communication networks
  • Efficient entanglement generation and fidelity optimization
  • Hybrid quantum-classical solvers for large-scale optimization

I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical quantum models and practical, implementable technologies.
